services

Olympic offers one way or round trip transportation services to local charter, private and public schools. We can transport your student rider from a convenient central location directly to your local school in a safe, clean, friendly and professional environment. Olympic is currently servicing Pine Lake Preparatory in Mooresville from various locations in the Huntersville and Cornelius areas. Please call for further details and information.

Olympic Transportation provides family friendly transportation to local venues, Charlotte area attractions, or just a day/evening trip out on the town. Our comfortable and clean shuttle can transport up to 14 passengers to your choice of locales. Travel can be reserved on a one way or round trip basis. Call Olympic with your trip details and we will be happy to provide you with a reasonable and economical way to transport your group.

Olympic is also a business friendly service provider. We can get your group of co-workers to and from that office meeting or seminar on-time and ready to go. Olympic can also offer your business a means of getting to your annual weekend business retreat in a safe and comfortable manner. Call for current pricing and availability.
